1.			      	  						   								 OVERVIEW 
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Thank you for choosing to install the Password_Vault application. 
We strive to ensure that your passwords and memorable information are as secure as possible when they are being handled by our application.
While our system is in use on your computer, we strive to ensure that your information is never stored anywhere other than your own personal computer. 
To prevent the theft of your secure information, we ensure that your computer is the only device able to decrypt your information using unique identifying information.
And to further protect that information, we ensure that even your unique identifying information is being secured with the latest hash algorithms whenever it is being stored. 
Please use the installation instructions below to continue.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2.			        						  							   INSTALLATION
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Extract the contents of the .zip folder.
2. Run the "setup_app" from the "setup" folder.
3. Allow admin permissions if prompted (this enables us to create shortcuts for you).
4. Follow the instructions through the installation wizard.
